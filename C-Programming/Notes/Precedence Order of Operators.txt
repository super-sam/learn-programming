OPERATORS ARE ARRANGED FROM HIGHER TO LOWER PRECEDENCE BELOW: 

	operators			Direction of Execution of Statement

1. !, +(unary), -(unary), ++, --		Right to Left
2. *, /, %				Left to Right
3. +, -					Left to Right
4. <, >, <=, >=				Left to Right
5. !=, ==					Left to Right
6. &&					Left to Right
7. ||					Left to Right
8. =, +=, -=, *=, /=, %= 			Right to Left
